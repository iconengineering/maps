ICON Engineering is a professional engineering consulting firm

  • 1997

    The Story Begins

    Doors open with 9 initial employees dedicated to stormwater solutions.

  • 2002

    FEMA CTP

    ICON and UDFCD pioneer local review of LOMRs to improve floodplain mapping.

  • 2013

    District Interns

    ICON's future begins with training new engineers. MHFD interns have been integral to our success!

  • 2016

    Stream Academy

    ICON started participating in the MHFD Stream Management Academy, riverSHARED training, Widland Hydrology (Rosgen) field courses and othe collaborative learning opportunities to advance our understanding and expertise in natural river systems.

  • 2022

    25 Years in the Books

    This will be a milestone year for ICON, as we look forward to more of the same in 2047!
